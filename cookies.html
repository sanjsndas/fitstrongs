Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how FitStrong Training ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. It describes what cookies are, how we use them, your choices regarding cookies, and how you can manage your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit websites. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, enhance your experience, and provide useful information to website owners.

Cookies may be stored temporarily as session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persist for longer periods as persistent cookies. They typically contain a unique identifier and other data related to your visit.

How We Use Cookies

At FitStrong Training, we use cookies for several purposes to improve our services and your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like navigation, secure areas, and user authentication.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website, such as pages visited and any errors encountered. This data helps us improve site functionality and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Functional cookies remember your preferences and settings, such as language choice or login information, to personalize your experience.
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: These cookies deliver relevant advertisements to you based on your interests and browsing habits, both on our site and other platforms.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are set by third-party service providers whom we use for analytics, advertising, and social media features. Examples include Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and others. We do not control these third parties' cookie practices. Please review their privacy and cookie policies on their websites.

Your Cookie Choices

You have the right to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Essential cookies are required for the website to function and cannot be declined.

Upon your first visit, you will see a cookie consent banner allowing you to accept or decline cookies. Your choice will be saved for future visits.

You can manage or withdraw your cookie consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or clearing cookies. Most browsers allow you to disable cookies or notify you when cookies are being set.

Why Cookies Are Important

Cookies improve your browsing experience by enabling features such as:

  • Keeping you logged in while navigating pages
  • Saving your preferences and settings
  • Analyzing website traffic to improve design and content
  • Serving personalized advertisements aligned with your interests

Without cookies, some website features may not work properly, and your experience may be less personalized.

Data Privacy and Cookies

While cookies collect information about your interaction with our website, they do not usually contain personal identification information unless you have provided one through other means.

We handle any data collected by cookies in accordance with our Privacy Policy, ensuring your data is protected and used responsibly.
