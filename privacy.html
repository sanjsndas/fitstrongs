Privacy Policy

At FitStrong Training, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website and use our services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services to you:

  • Personal Identification Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, postal address, and other details you provide when signing up or contacting us.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website such as pages visited, time spent, clicks, and device information collected through cookies and tracking technologies.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase membership or services, your payment details will be processed through secure third-party payment processors. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.
  • Communication Data: Any messages, inquiries, or feedback you send to us during your interactions.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our fitness training services and website.
  • To personalize your experience and improve our content and offerings.
  • To process your transactions and send you related information such as confirmations and receipts.
  • To communicate with you, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support.
  • To send you promotional materials and updates about new programs, events, and offers with your consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data

We process your personal data only when we have a legitimate reason to do so, such as:

  • With your consent to send you marketing communications.
  • To fulfill our contract with you for providing fitness services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect your vital interests or those of another person.

Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. However, we may share your information in the following ways:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors and partners who help us operate the website, process payments, or provide services, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or governmental authority, we may disclose information to comply with legal processes, investigations, or regulatory requirements.
  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales, your information may be transferred as part of the business assets.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or marketing.
  • Restriction: Request us to restrict processing in certain circumstances.
  • Data Portability: Request your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for processing at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@fitstrongtraining.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze usage, and deliver personalized content. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect some website features.

We use cookies for:

  • Essential functionalities such as user authentication and security.
  • Performance monitoring and analytics to understand user behavior.
  • Personalization of content and marketing communications (with consent).

Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

However, no online transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure, so while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. When your personal data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, advertisements, or services that are not controlled by us. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies when visiting them.
